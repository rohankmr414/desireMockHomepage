PROJECT ADHYAYAN

Adhyayn strives to provide equal educational opportunities to the children of EWS of the society under the ambit of RTE Act 12(1)(c) by using the youth as a resource.

KHWAISH-E-SHIKSHA

Books for primary standard students from a financially weaker section of the society were arranged by exchanging books from all over the community that were not required by the people anymore.

LITER OF LIGHT

In 2015, Desire Foundation took steps to illuminate houses in the slums of Bhubaneshwar which were still in darkness. Originated in Brazil, this is an eco-friendly and long-lasting way to light homes!

About

Desire Foundation is a govt. reg. trust which aims to empower children from vulnerable sections of the society by providing them equal opportunities for education.

Currently, we are working on implementation of RTE Sec 12(1)(c) in Bhubaneshwar through our project Adhyayan which has Successfully completed phase 1, 2 & 3. Phase 3 completed on April 2019.

Over these five years we have interacted with 1030 children, 30 schools and over 150 young students.
